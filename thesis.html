My thesis for the Master's Computer Engineering degree involved the creation of a telemedicine system to support patients suffering from intestinal failure. In particular, the work consisted in the creation of a cross-platform mobile application for patients using Google's Flutter, a server (NodeJS + PostgreSQL + Redis) and a web application for physicians (React).

The thesis is written in English and called "NAD-APP: Analysis and Development of a Telemedicine System for Patients Suffering from Intestinal Failure".

Supervisors: Prof. Paolo Bellavista, Prof. Loris Pironi

For my Bachelor's Computer Engineering degree, I developed a Gesture Recognition System and my thesis was focused on the research of the right Machine Learning algorithms, the creation of the motion tracking devices and the design of a fully custumizable modular system.

The thesis is written in Italian and called "Sviluppo di un sistema di Gesture Recognition".
